About Felora Design

Felora Design stands proudly as an illustrious international fashion company with its headquarters based in the vibrant city of Tehran, Iran. Our exquisite services span across continents, catering to fashion enthusiasts worldwide. With dedicated service providers in countries such as United States, England, Canada, Australia, Germany, we keep striving to expand our operation in more countries in order to seamlessly connect cultures through the art of fashion.

At the heart of our extraordinary journey is the visionary and creative force behind Felora Design, none other than the esteemed Mrs. Felora Soleymanpour. With an unwavering passion for fashion and an exceptional talent in design, Mrs. Soleymanpour has been instrumental in shaping the brand's identity and global presence.

With an illustrious career spanning over four decades, Mrs. Soleymanpour's remarkable contributions to the fashion industry have left an indelible mark. From envisioning timeless collections to perfecting intricate designs, she has truly redefined elegance and sophistication in the world of haute couture.

About Mrs. Soleymanpour and her early experiences in the design world that go beyond her four decades as a professional designer, her journey in the designing career began during her high school years. The high school she attended had a uniform policy, but she was the only student exempted from wearing the standard uniform. Instead, she used the same fabric and color as other students to design a custom-made outfit for herself. This outfit was still identical to the school uniform but tailored to fit her preferences and showcased her unique style. The school principal approved this modification, making an exception for her to express her artistry through her uniform.

Felora Design proudly carries the legacy of Mrs. Soleymanpour's unparalleled dedication to craftsmanship and attention to detail. Her unique designs have adorned numerous satisfied clients, who have come to rely on her creations for a multitude of special occasions. From intimate wedding nights to glamorous galas, Felora Design has become synonymous with sophistication and opulence.

A testament to Mrs. Soleymanpour's artistic brilliance is the recent collaboration with the spectacular film "The Ritual" (in Persian: "مراسم ویژه"), which eloquently depicts the inspiring journey of Mr. Nelson Mandela in South Africa. This cinematic marvel features the radiant Mrs. Mahtab Keramati, a beacon of Persian talent, and is skillfully directed by the brilliant Mr. Meysam Shahbabaee.

As Felora Design continues to dazzle the fashion world, we honor and celebrate the extraordinary accomplishments of Mrs. Felora Soleymanpour. Her artistic vision and tireless efforts continue to elevate our brand to new heights, leaving an indelible impression on the hearts of our cherished clientele.

With an unwavering commitment to quality and creativity, Felora Design invites you to embark on a magnificent journey like no other. Join us as we continue to weave dreams into reality, empowering men and women with exquisite designs that capture the essence of beauty and allure.
